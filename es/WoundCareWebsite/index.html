Meet Dr. Martin, FNP

Dr. Martin is a highly experienced Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) whose clinical approach is built on a rare and powerful foundation: two decades of high-level surgical expertise combined with compassionate, patient-centered nursing care.

His primary goal is to significantly improve his patients' quality of life through meticulous, evidence-based wound management, delivered right to your home.

Why his background matters for Wound Care: His advanced residency in neurosurgery provides unparalleled insight into complex physiology and surgical healing principles—an invaluable advantage when treating non-healing and chronic wounds.

Credentials & Expertise:

  • Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) - Graduate of Miami Regional University (2023)
  • General Practitioner & Neurosurgeon - Graduated from the University of Medical Sciences of Havana (2006)
  • Over 20 years of clinical experience, including specialized surgical training
  • Extensive experience in high-acuity settings (e.g., Spring Valley Hospital, Horizon Specialty Hospital)
  • Dedicated to mobile, personalized, and compassionate patient care
